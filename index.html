THE RECIPE

Time Stamps:

  • Time: 2 Minutes
  • Cook time: 2+ Minutes
  • Servings: 1-2

Ingredients:

  • 1 Packet of maggi
  • 1 Chopped Onion
  • 1 Chopped Tomato
  • 2 Chopped Bell pepper
  • 2 TableSpoon oil

Directions:

  • Add oil in a fry pan
  • Cook onion in high flame.
  • Combine onions and tomato, then add masala mix, cook for a few more minutes.
  • Add bell peppers.
